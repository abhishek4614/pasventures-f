Graphic Design

We begin this article by answering the basic question i.e., what is graphic designing? Graphic designing is an art where images, illustrations and typography and formats are used to convey messages. One of the basic requirements for a platform to flourish and grown online is graphic designing. For a customer to be attracted to an online platform, the prerequisite is that the user interface should be attractive and attention seeking. Graphic designing is the way illustrations are represented on UIs and UXs. The aesthetic appeal and marketing efficacy of a business online is determined by graphic designing. It is the job of graphic designers to listen to a business’s concerns and to understand their goals of how they would like to represent themselves on an online platform. The graphic designer works to accentuate the ability of a business UI or UX. It is the graphic design that determines the usability, accessibility and user-friendliness of a business online.

Graphic designing is a technique used by graphic designers to attract viewers using fonts, illustrations and colours. When developing graphic designs, designers are recommended to use human-centric approach. The graphic, typography, fonts, illustration, etc., must be such that they evoke human emotions and attract a person towards the platform or user-interface. The placement of images, the way fonts are selected and the way illustrations are designed, should connect to the purpose and vision of the client’s business and to the emotional affinity of the respective potential customer.

Product Development

Human capital of an organization is the most important element in it. Human capital or Human resource is that resource which operates other resources in the organization. Hence, HR Services are extremely important for the proper functioning and survival of the organization.

Human resource planning helps to fill the gap between the demand of employees in the organization and the supply of labour from the outside world. Human resource planning is done by the HR executives in order to understand the need or requirement of employees that an organization might have presently or in the future with the increase of job portfolios in the organization. While performing the HR service of HRP, an HR executive performs job analysis and job description which paves the way for HR functions such as recruitment, selection and training and development. The HR Service of HRP also takes into consideration the amount of compensation that must be provided for a job in the company based on its analysis and the description.

HR Services provided by an organization include the following:

  • HRP (Human Resource planning).
  • Recruitment.
  • Selection.
  • Performance management.
  • Administrative functions.
  • Training and Development.
  • Performance Evaluation.
  • Compensation.
  • Industrial Relations.
  • Health and Well-being of employees.
